Modular Bone Conduction Hearing Aids







Intro & Research


Over 5% of the world’s population and approximately one third of people over 65 years of age are affected by disabling hearing loss. Bone conduction hearing aid captures sound and transfers it through bone vibration to the cochlea.

The existing designs are mostly headset or glasses style. I aim to design a hearing aid that's beautiful and can satisfy user's need in different scenerios throughout the day.
