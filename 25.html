I found myself easily get distracted when I work on my computer, so I built this desktop app to help me to be more focused.

Setting a clear intention is helpful. The app asks me to write down a very specific and small task to focus on for the next 25 minutes before start working. After 25 minutes, the app will encourage me to take a break. If I choose to continue working, the app keeps tracking the time that I spend on this task. When I finish, I can update what I have done based on my plan. The app has a record of how I spent my time.

I developed this app with electron.js.
